Welcome! I am a Senior Data Scientist at Department of Public Health, County of Los Angeles As a Data Scientist at DPH, I work to develop machine learning models for public health sector. Before joining DPH, I worked as a Senior Data Scientist for BlackLine Systems, inc. My research work focuses on innovating and designing applications using machine learning, specifically deep learning technologies. I am passionate about creating efficient solutions to solve challenging problems in interdisciplinary research areas. My research interests also includes computer vision, medical image analysis, mHealth, and software engineering.

I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science in August 2019, from Georgia State University. My advisor was Prof. Yanqing Zhang. My dissertation title is "Towards AI-Assisted Disease Diagnosis: Learning Deep Feature Representations for Medical Image Analysis"

I was also a Brain and Behavior (B&B) Fellow of The Neuroscience Institute at Georgia State University. As a B&B fellow, I worked to develop automated Computer-aided diagnosis (CAD) systems able to diagnose neurological disorders. I was a student ambassador for Intel. As a selected AI Student Ambassador, I worked on different projects collaboratively with Intel focusing on medical image analysis applying deep learning technologies.

I joined GSU at fall 2014. Before that, I was a Senior Software Engineer in Samsung R&D Institute Bangladesh. At Samsung R&D, I worked to develop an Automated Test Tool for Android application. Besides I worked in the S-voice and media-player app developer team. Before joining Samsung R&D Institute, I was a Senior Software Engineer at Together Initiatives Limited. I worked in developing several enterprise applications and worked extensively in J2EE environment. Besides, I was involved in mobile health research based software development. Along with my team, I worked for developing "MHealth: Empowering CHWs to Improve Maternal, Newborn and Child Health" for BRAC.

I obtained my Bachelors and Masters degree in Computer Science and Engineering from University of Dhaka, Bangladesh.
